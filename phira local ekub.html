What is the Ekub System?

An Ekub is a deeply trusted traditional Ethiopian financial rotation association that serves as an alternative savings mechanism. Instead of relying on formal banking lines, a dedicated group of peers pool their capital together to build up their collective financial growth safely.

🔄

The Rotation Scheme

Every set interval (either weekly or monthly), members contribute a fixed sum of money. The collected dynamic total lump-sum pool is awarded to one chosen member each turn until everyone wins.

⚖️

Penalty Enforcement

To preserve collective trust, timely cycles are vital. Missing your cycle deadlines imposes a 10% penalty fee. This extra amount buffers administrative costs and protects group reliability.

🛡️

Digital Orderly Tracking

Our online portal solves administrative confusion. It instantly creates a digital unique ID sequence track for users, manages billing transparently, and rings real-time alerts for system administrators.
